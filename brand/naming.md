# HAIKU Brand Constants

## Name

- **Full name:** HAIKU
- **Expanded:** Human AI Knowledge Unification
- **Always written:** ALL CAPS (HAIKU), never "Haiku" when referring to the framework

## Tagline Options

1. **"Disciplined form. Unified intelligence."**
2. **"Structure that scales from intent to insight."**
3. **"Constrained form. Clear outcomes."**

## Domain

- **Primary:** haikumethod.ai

## Relationships

- **AI-DLC** is the software development profile of HAIKU. It implements HAIKU's 4-phase lifecycle with git workflows, test suites, pull requests, CI/CD pipelines, and deployment gates.
- **SWARM** (Scope, Workstreams, Accountability, Results, Memory) validates HAIKU's universality by applying the framework to marketing and sales domains.
- **HAIKU** is the universal core --- AI-DLC and SWARM are domain-specific profiles that inherit its lifecycle, principles, and collaboration model.

## Visual Identity Direction

- Minimal and clean
- Japanese-inspired aesthetic sensibility
- Clean typography with generous whitespace
- Restrained color palette --- monochrome with selective accent
- Structural motifs reflecting constraint and clarity (grids, balanced composition)
- No decorative excess; every element serves a purpose
