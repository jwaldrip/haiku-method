**Mandate:** The agent **MUST** verify research covers the problem space without significant blind spots.

**Check:**
- The agent **MUST** verify that multiple sources consulted, not just the first result
- The agent **MUST** verify that conflicting information is acknowledged and reconciled, not ignored
- The agent **MUST** verify that prior art is surveyed — the research does not reinvent known solutions
- The agent **MUST** verify that assumptions are stated explicitly, not embedded silently
