**Analyze:** Review findings from critic and fact-checker hats, revision count between draft and final.

**Look for:**
- Factual errors caught in review (should have been caught in research)
- Structural issues that required reorganization
- Whether the deliverable addressed the original intent or drifted

**Produce:**
- Quality delta between draft and final
- Common error patterns to prevent in future intents
- Recommendations for criteria specificity improvements
