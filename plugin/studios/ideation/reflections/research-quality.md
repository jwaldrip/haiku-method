**Analyze:** Breadth and depth of sources consulted, research gaps identified during creation.

**Look for:**
- Were multiple perspectives represented or was research one-sided?
- Did the create stage uncover gaps the research stage missed?
- Were assumptions validated or just carried forward?

**Produce:**
- Research thoroughness assessment
- Gaps identified retrospectively
- Recommendations for research methodology improvements
