**Analyze:** User research predictions vs actual user behavior, competitive positioning accuracy.

**Look for:**
- User needs that research identified but the roadmap didn't address
- Competitive threats that materialized vs were overestimated
- Whether user segments behaved as the research predicted
- Market assumptions that proved wrong

**Produce:**
- Research-to-reality comparison
- Competitive positioning accuracy assessment
- Recommendations for research methodology improvements
