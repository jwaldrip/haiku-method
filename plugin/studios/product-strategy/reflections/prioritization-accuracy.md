**Analyze:** Did the highest-priority items deliver the predicted impact? Were deprioritized items correctly deprioritized?

**Look for:**
- Items that were prioritized high but delivered low impact (overestimated)
- Items that were deprioritized but later became urgent (underestimated)
- Whether the scoring framework produced defensible rankings
- Stakeholder overrides that improved or worsened outcomes

**Produce:**
- Prioritization accuracy assessment
- Scoring framework calibration recommendations
- Recommendations for reducing stakeholder bias in prioritization
