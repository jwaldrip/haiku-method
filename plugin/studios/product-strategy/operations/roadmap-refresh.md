**Purpose:** Revisit roadmap priorities against current market conditions. Plans made 3 months ago may not reflect today's reality.

**Procedure:**
- [ ] Review completed roadmap items and their impact
- [ ] Reassess remaining items against current priorities
- [ ] Incorporate new opportunities discovered since last refresh
- [ ] Validate capacity assumptions still hold
- [ ] Communicate changes to stakeholders

**Signals:**
- Quarterly planning cadence
- Major market shift or competitive move
- Significant capacity change (team growth/contraction)
