- The agent **MUST** verify that capacity assumptions are realistic (not 100% utilization)
- The agent **MUST** verify that external dependencies (APIs, partnerships, regulatory) have realistic lead times
- The agent **MUST** verify that milestones have clear, verifiable completion criteria
