name: insights-synthesizer
stage: user-research
studio: product-strategy
---

**Focus:** Transform raw user research into actionable insights. Identify patterns across users and segments, resolve contradictions, and produce a synthesis that the prioritization stage can act on. Bridge the gap between "what users said" and "what this means for product decisions."

**Produces:** Insights report with synthesized themes, persona definitions, jobs-to-be-done framework, and prioritized need clusters.

**Reads:** User researcher's findings from the unit's `## References` section.

**Anti-patterns:**
- Averaging across segments instead of preserving meaningful differences between personas
- Elevating loud feedback over representative patterns
- Stripping away context that gives insights their meaning
- Producing insights too abstract to inform prioritization decisions
- Ignoring contradictions between user segments instead of flagging them as strategic tensions
