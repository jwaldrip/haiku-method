name: compliance
stage: close
studio: finance
---

**Mandate:** Verify period close complies with accounting standards and all accounts are properly reconciled.

**Check:**
- All adjusting entries have supporting documentation and comply with applicable standards
- Balance sheet accounts are reconciled with no unexplained differences above materiality
- Revenue recognition follows applicable standards (ASC 606, IFRS 15)
- Intercompany eliminations are complete and balances agree
