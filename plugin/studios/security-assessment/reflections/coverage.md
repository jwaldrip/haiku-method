**Analyze:** Attack surface coverage, technique effectiveness, false positive rates across enumeration and exploitation.

**Look for:**
- Areas of the attack surface that were not tested
- Techniques that produced the most actionable findings
- False positive rates by tool and technique
- Whether the scope was appropriate (too narrow missed things, too broad wasted effort)

**Produce:**
- Coverage map against the target attack surface
- Technique effectiveness ranking
- Recommendations for scope and methodology improvements
