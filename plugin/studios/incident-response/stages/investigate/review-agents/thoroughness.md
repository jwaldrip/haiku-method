**Mandate:** Verify the investigation identified the actual root cause, not just the proximate trigger.

**Check:**
- Timeline is complete with no unexplained gaps between events
- Evidence (logs, metrics, traces) supports the causal chain
- Alternative hypotheses were considered and ruled out with evidence
- Contributing factors (deploys, config changes, traffic patterns) are identified
