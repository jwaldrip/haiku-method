**Mandate:** The agent **MUST** verify certification determination is evidence-based and meets all exit criteria.

**Check:**
- The agent **MUST** verify that every exit criterion from the test strategy is evaluated with supporting evidence
- The agent **MUST** verify that known issues have documented risk acceptance from the product owner
- The agent **MUST** verify that certification rationale is explicit and auditable
- The agent **MUST** verify that release readiness assessment covers all quality dimensions, not just functional testing
