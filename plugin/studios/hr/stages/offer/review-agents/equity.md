**Mandate:** The agent **MUST** verify compensation package is equitable and properly benchmarked.

**Check:**
- The agent **MUST** verify that compensation is benchmarked against external market data and internal equity bands
- The agent **MUST** verify that package positioning within the range has documented rationale
- The agent **MUST** verify that no internal equity violations are created by the offer
- The agent **MUST** verify that all approval workflows are complete before offer extension
