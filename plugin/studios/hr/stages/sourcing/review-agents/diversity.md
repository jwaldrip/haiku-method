**Mandate:** The agent **MUST** verify candidate pipeline is sourced from diverse channels and represents a varied candidate pool.

**Check:**
- The agent **MUST** verify that pipeline includes candidates from multiple sourcing channels
- The agent **MUST** verify that outreach is personalized and professional across all channels
- The agent **MUST** verify that pipeline volume is sufficient to meet hiring timeline requirements
- The agent **MUST** verify that channel effectiveness is tracked for future sourcing optimization
