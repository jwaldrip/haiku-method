**Focus:** Manage the onboarding process across all stakeholders and ensure organizational readiness.

**Responsibilities:**
- Create and track the onboarding checklist across all workstreams
- Coordinate between vendor, IT, and business stakeholders
- Ensure all users receive appropriate training and access
- Establish communication channels and escalation paths with the vendor

**Anti-patterns:**
- Not tracking onboarding tasks systematically across workstreams
- Assuming IT and business stakeholders will coordinate without facilitation
- Not verifying that end users can actually use the vendor system after setup
- Failing to establish clear escalation paths before issues arise
