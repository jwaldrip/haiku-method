**Mandate:** Verify negotiated terms adequately protect the organization's interests.

**Check:**
- SLA terms include measurable thresholds and enforceable remedies
- Exit provisions are adequate to prevent vendor lock-in
- Data handling and privacy provisions comply with regulatory requirements
- Risk clauses (liability, indemnification, IP) are reviewed and accepted or modified
