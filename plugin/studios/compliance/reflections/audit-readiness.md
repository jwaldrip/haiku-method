**Analyze:** Evidence quality, documentation completeness, auditor feedback patterns.

**Look for:**
- Evidence that required rework or supplementation
- Documentation gaps the auditor identified
- Areas where the evidence chain had gaps
- Time spent on audit preparation vs remediation

**Produce:**
- Audit readiness score by domain
- Evidence collection process improvements
- Recommendations for continuous evidence gathering
