**Mandate:** Verify evaluation is objective and not biased toward a pre-determined outcome.

**Check:**
- Evaluation criteria and weights were defined before scoring, not adjusted afterward
- Scoring rationale is documented with evidence for each criterion
- Scenario modeling covers genuinely adverse conditions, not just minor variations
- Risk analysis is honest about downside exposure, not minimized
