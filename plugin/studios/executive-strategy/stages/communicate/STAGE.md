# Communicate

## Criteria Guidance

Good criteria examples:
- "Communication plan tailors messaging to each stakeholder group with specific channels, timing, and key messages"
- "Rollout plan sequences actions with dependencies, owners, and measurable milestones"
- "FAQ document anticipates the top 10 likely questions with pre-approved responses"

Bad criteria examples:
- "Communication is planned"
- "Stakeholders are informed"
- "Rollout is ready"

## Completion Signal (RFC 2119)

Communications package **MUST** exist with stakeholder-specific messaging, rollout plan with sequenced actions and ownership, and FAQ for anticipated questions. Communicator **MUST** have confirmed messaging is consistent across audiences and channels. Planner **MUST** have validated the rollout sequence is feasible and dependencies **MUST** be identified.
