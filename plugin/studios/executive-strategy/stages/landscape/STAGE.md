# Landscape

## Criteria Guidance

Good criteria examples:
- "Market analysis identifies at least 5 trends with evidence-based assessment of their impact on the organization"
- "Competitive intelligence maps each competitor's strategic position, recent moves, and likely next actions"
- "SWOT analysis connects each element to specific, verifiable facts rather than generic observations"

Bad criteria examples:
- "Market is understood"
- "Competition is analyzed"
- "Landscape is mapped"

## Completion Signal (RFC 2119)

Landscape analysis **MUST** exist with market trends documented, competitive positions mapped, and organizational strengths and vulnerabilities assessed against the strategic question. Strategist **MUST** have framed the decision context. Analyst **MUST** have **MUST** be verified data sources and validated the analytical framework.
