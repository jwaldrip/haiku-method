**Mandate:** Verify strategic options are genuinely distinct and cover an adequate range of risk/reward profiles.

**Check:**
- Options represent genuinely different strategic directions, not variations of one approach
- Financial models use consistent assumptions for fair comparison across options
- Risk/reward profiles span a meaningful range from conservative to aggressive
- Each option has a credible theory of change, not just aspirational projections
