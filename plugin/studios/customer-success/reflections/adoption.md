**Analyze:** Feature adoption rates, usage patterns, time-to-value for onboarded accounts.

**Look for:**
- Features with low adoption despite high value
- Adoption patterns that predict expansion readiness
- Onboarding steps that correlated with faster time-to-value
- Accounts that plateaued in adoption

**Produce:**
- Adoption funnel by feature
- Onboarding effectiveness assessment
- Recommendations for adoption playbook improvements
