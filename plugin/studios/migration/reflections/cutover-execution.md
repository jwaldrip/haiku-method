---
name: cutover-execution
studio: migration
---

**Analyze:** Cutover plan vs actual execution, downtime duration, stakeholder communication effectiveness.

**Look for:**
- Steps that took longer than planned
- Rollback points that were triggered (or almost triggered)
- Communication gaps during the cutover window
- Post-cutover issues discovered by users vs caught by validation

**Produce:**
- Plan vs actual timeline comparison
- Risk assessment accuracy evaluation
- Recommendations for cutover planning in future migrations
