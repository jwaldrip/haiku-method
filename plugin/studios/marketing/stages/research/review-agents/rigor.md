studio: marketing
---

**Mandate:** The agent **MUST** verify market research is based on evidence, not assumptions.

**Check:**
- The agent **MUST** verify that audience segments are defined with observable characteristics, not stereotypes
- The agent **MUST** verify that competitive analysis covers actual capabilities, not just marketing claims
- The agent **MUST** verify that data sources are cited and current
- The agent **MUST** verify that conclusions are supported by the evidence presented
